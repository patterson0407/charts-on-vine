Policies

Welcome to Charters on Vine! We are excited to have you join us for one of our unique and fun events. Please take a moment to review our policies for a safe and enjoyable experience.

Ticket Policies

  • All ticket purchases are non-refundable. Cancellations made within 72 hours of the scheduled departure are non-exchangeable.
  • Third-party ticket sales are not authorized.

Child Safety

  • The trolley does not have child safety seats. Children 2 and under may ride securely on a parent's lap.
  • All children over the age of 2 require a ticket.

Alcohol Policy

  • Alcohol is allowed on the trolley. No glass containers are permitted.

Respectful Behavior

  • Please respect the Charters on Vine team and fellow guests. Unruly behavior may result in removal from the trolley.

Age-Restricted Events

  • Some tours are for guests 21 and over.

Liability and Responsibility

  • If you cause damage to the trolley, you will be responsible for repair costs.
  • Charters on Vine is not responsible for lost or stolen belongings.

Weather Policy

  • In dangerous weather conditions, events may be rescheduled or refunded. Dangerous weather includes icy roads, significant snow, or tornado conditions.

Photography

  • Photos and videos may be taken during the event for marketing purposes. If you wish not to participate, notify your host at the start of the event.

Chartered Events

  • Chartered events require a minimum of 2-hour rental.
